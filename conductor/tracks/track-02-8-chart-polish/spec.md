# Track 02.8: Chart Refinement

**Goal:** Polish the visual appearance of the Weekly Fasting Chart.

## Scope
1.  **Columns:**
    -   Top-rounded corners (instead of pill).
    -   Adjust width/spacing.
2.  **Axes:**
    -   Remove vertical axis line (keep labels).
    -   Add dashed horizontal guidelines.
3.  **Colors:**
    -   Use a consistent brand green.
    -   Ensure high contrast for text.

## Success Criteria
-   Chart looks modern and professional.
-   Data is clearly readable.
